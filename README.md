📖 Project Overview
Survival of Travel: Session 1 - Trip to Mars is an immersive multiplayer game developed for Roblox that simulates the journey to Mars. Players take on the role of crew members aboard a spacecraft, facing realistic challenges that astronauts encounter during deep space missions.

"We wanted to build a game that allows you to experience a trip to Mars in the most accurate way possible with respect to the challenges of the journey and its dangers, in addition to showing the activities that astronauts carry out when they are on a mission." 

Our goal is to use gaming as a powerful tool for scientific dissemination, making space exploration accessible and engaging for a wide audience.

🌟 Key Features
4-6 Player Co-op: Work together as a crew to survive the journey

Real-time Statistics: Manage Hunger, Strength, and Mental Health

Dynamic Challenges: Face cosmic rays, solar particle events, and system failures

Ship Systems Management: Maintain life support and critical systems

Scientific Accuracy: Based on real NASA research and astronaut experiences

🎮 Gameplay
Players must manage their personal well-being while maintaining the ship's systems. Each decision impacts the mission's success:

Personal Stats: Monitor hunger, physical condition, and mental state

Ship Maintenance: Repair and maintain critical systems

External Threats: React to cosmic events and space hazards

Team Coordination: Cooperate with crew members to survive

The ship has key systems that at a certain level of deterioration (due to external causes, misuse of tools, or lack of maintenance) increase risks to the health of the crew, causing the imminent death of the player and mission failure.

🛠️ Development
Why Roblox?
We chose the Roblox platform for three key factors:

Massiveness: Large user base to reach a greater number of people

Support: Provides tools to create multiplayer online games, free and easy to integrate

Age Range: Matches perfectly with the target audience posed by the challenge

Development Tools
Roblox Studio — 3D environment development

Lua — Object-oriented programming

Android Apps — Picsart, Video Maker, Logo Maker, CapCut for graphic design

🏆 Awards & Recognition
Global Nominee — NASA Space Apps Challenge 2021

📝 Hackathon Journey
"It was a great experience since we were able to integrate two areas (Programming and Visual and Graphic Arts) that we personally had a hard time complementing in other events." 

Our approach was to create a game based on the experience of a real trip to space, conducting in-depth research on astronaut engineers who had the opportunity to be part of the International Space Station team.

Special thanks to the Mars Society team for their invaluable help, experience, and advice to improve our final project.
